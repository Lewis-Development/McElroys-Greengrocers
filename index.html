McElroy’s Greengrocers

REFRESHINGLY LOCAL

Under New Management

Over the past thirty-two years McElroys Greengrocers has established itself as one of East Belfast’s most popular and well known traditional fruit & vegetable shops. It is located in Ballyhackamore on the Upper Newtownards Road, Belfast, with its busy and vibrant village atmosphere and offers a full range of fresh seasonal locally sourced produce. Now under new management, McElroys continues to specialise in stocking competitively priced and high quality fruits and vegetables, as well as exotics with an emphasis on purchasing from local farmers and suppliers.

In order to ensure the freshest produce, our deliveries are brought in on a daily basis as well as sourced from the market daily. We offer a free delivery service to our local customers within a four mile radius. So why not call us on (+44) 028 9047 1966 and speak to our friendly and helpful staff.

We supply many businesses in Belfast and beyond including local nursing homes, cafes, shops, offices and restaurants.

We visit the market five days a week to ensure that our produce is always fresh. We are proud to support local suppliers and have a number of farmers who supply us with fresh, local and seasonal produce. To name one, John Hamilton from Comber, Co. Down, supplies us all year round with vegetables and potatoes. We have been selling his Comber Earlies for well over twenty years. Another local product which proves popular is Philip Earls' local honey which we have delivered fresh every week. People come from all over Belfast especially to buy it. John Sloan from Lisburn provides us with his carrots and parsnips which we start selling as soon as they are harvested. Over the summer months we get our Strawberries from Seamus Donnelly in Dungannon, as he has both early and late crops. Later victoria plums arrive. Local peas, broad beans and beetroot are all popular.

PRODUCTS AVAILABLE AT MCELROY'S QUALITY GREENGROCERS

We stock a variety of vegetables, fruit and general groceries in our shop in Ballyhackamore

  • Fresh Fruit
  • We stock a large selection of fresh fruit from local farms and wholesalers as well as a wide variety of fresh exotic fruits including Chinese Pears.
  • Fresh Vegetables
  • We stock a large selection of vegetables from local farms and wholesalers including broccoli, cauliflower, cabbage, parsnips and leeks.
  • Potatoes
  • We can supply a variety of potatoes throughout the year from the early Comber varieties - Queens and Accord to the later Maris Pypers and Navan.
  • Fresh Herbs & Spices
  • We order in a wide variety of fresh, loose herbs every day and repackage them into convenient packs for our customers.
  • Fresh Eggs
  • There is a full range of small, medium and large eggs both in our caged hen packs as well as free-range eggs. Any egg you pick will be fresh and tasteful.
  • Fresh Duck Eggs
  • Our fresh Duck Eggs from Rams Island Farm, Lough Neagh are delivered on a regular basis to our store. These eggs are sure to be full of taste.
  • County Down Honey
  • Our local Honey, supplied from Philip Earle in Comber, Co. Down, is very popular with all those who walk through our doors.
  • Specialist Chutneys
  • Mrs. Darlington Chutneys Autumn Fruit Chutney is packed full of plums, apples, onions, tomatoes and spices that have all been simmered slowly.
  • Local Jams & Preserves
  • Moyrah Jams, Homemade in Craigavon is full of Strawberries, Raspberries, Blackcurrant, Damson, Rhubarb, Ginger, Victoria Plums & Gooseberries.
  • Local Marmalades
  • We stock local marmalades from Co. Down & Craigavon. Orange, grapefruit & lemon, orange & lemon, whiskey, brandy and 3 fruit marmalade.
  • Nuts and Dried Fruit
  • We stock a wide variety of nuts and dried fruit from our local supplier, Viva, for that wholesome snack that we all love.
  • Organic Stock Cubes
  • Kallo Organic Stock Cubes have no artificial colours, flavours or preservatives. Vegetarian & vegan friendly also gluten and lactose free.
  • Barnhill Apple Juices
  • Barnhill Apple Juice is a delicious blend of varieties grown in Co. Armagh. It is made from whole fruit with no added sweeteners or colourings.
  • Barr’s Butchers Deli
  • We stock products from Barr’s Butchers Deli including delicious soups and packs of fresh pre-packed vegetables for your own perfect bowl.
  • Pre-Packed Vegetables
  • For your convenience we are now supplying our own freshly prepared vegetable packs including stir fry, fine beans and crispy salad.
